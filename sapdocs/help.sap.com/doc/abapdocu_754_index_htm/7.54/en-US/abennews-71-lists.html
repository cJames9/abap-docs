Lists in Release 7.0, EhP2


1. Support for the new 12 hour format



Modification 1

Support for the New 12 Hour Format

The new addition ENVIRONMENT TIME FORMAT to the WRITE TO and WRITE statements supports the new 12 hour format.

The predefined time formats of the statements WRITE TO and WRITE cannot be influenced automatically due to downward compatibility.

Note

This modification was also added to Release 7.0 in SP14.
